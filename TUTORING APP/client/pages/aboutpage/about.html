About Our Tutoring Platform

Connecting students with qualified tutors for academic success

Our Mission

TutorConnect was created to simplify the process of finding academic help on campus. We believe that every student deserves access to quality tutoring from peers who understand the specific challenges of their courses and institution.

How It Works

For Tutors

  • Create detailed profiles showcasing your expertise
  • Select your school and subjects you can tutor
  • Set your own hourly rates based on your experience
  • Manage your availability with our flexible calendar
  • Communicate directly with potential students
  • Receive payments through Venmo, PayPal, or cash

For Students

  • Browse tutors from your own school
  • Filter by subject, rating, and availability
  • Book sessions that fit your schedule
  • Communicate with tutors via our messaging system
  • Pay securely through preferred payment methods
  • Leave reviews to help other students
